Activity: Disability Simulation

Physical Impairments

  • Splints for strokes and spinal cord injuries
  • Gloves to simulate arthritis
  • Head sticks for spinal cord injuries

Color Blindness

  • Protanopia: No red cones.
  • Deuteranopia: No green cones.
  • Tritanopia: No blue cones.
  • Monochromacy: No color perception.

Common Visual Impairments

  • Glaucoma: Peripheral vision loss.
  • Refractive Errors: Blurry vision.
  • Cataracts: Loss of contrast and visual field obstruction.
  • Diabetic Retinopathy: Visual field obstruction.
  • Macula Degeneration: Central vision loss, loss of color perception.
